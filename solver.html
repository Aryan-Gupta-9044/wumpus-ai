AI Solution Path

The AI solver for Wumpus World uses knowledge-based reasoning to navigate the world safely. It keeps track of what it knows and what it can infer based on its percepts.

How the AI Reasons:

  1. The AI maintains a knowledge base of what it knows about each cell in the grid.
  2. When it perceives a breeze, it knows that at least one adjacent cell contains a pit.
  3. When it perceives a stench, it knows that at least one adjacent cell contains the Wumpus.
  4. As it explores, it updates its knowledge and infers which cells are safe to visit.
  5. It uses a search algorithm to find an optimal path to the gold while avoiding dangers.

Logical Reasoning Example:

If the AI is at position (1,1) and perceives no breeze and no stench, it can infer that:

  • All adjacent cells (0,1), (1,0), (1,2), (2,1) are safe from both pits and the Wumpus.
  • This information is added to the knowledge base.
  • The AI can now safely move to any of these cells without risk.

If the AI then moves to (1,2) and perceives a breeze but no stench, it can infer that:

  • At least one of the adjacent cells (0,2), (1,3), (2,2) contains a pit.
  • It knows (1,1) is safe (it just came from there).
  • It will update its knowledge base with this information.
  • The AI will avoid unproven cells until it has more information.

Through this logical reasoning process, the AI can navigate the Wumpus World safely and efficiently to find the gold.
